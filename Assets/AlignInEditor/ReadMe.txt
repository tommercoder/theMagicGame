How To Use

1) Select one or more objects in the Hirachy.
2) Click the "Add Component" button and add the "AlignInEditor" component.
3) To preview the position of the object(s) click the checkbox "Show Line To Surface".
4) Click the "Align" checkbox to align the object(s).
5) Remove the "AlignInEditor" component.
